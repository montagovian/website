BasedBench: evaluating meme understanding in VLMs

How based is your model?

Give a VLM an image of a meme or visual joke. What does it mean? That's it... that's the tweet task.

Task source

Inspired by SWE-Bench, BasedBench finds a task and it's validator out in the wild: in "explain the joke" subreddits where people ask earnestly what a meme or visual joke means and commenters sometimes oblige and explain the joke.

Ground truth

The BasedBench pipeline filters down to those posts with a critical mass of comments supporting a shared interpretation -- thereby taken to be ground truth.
